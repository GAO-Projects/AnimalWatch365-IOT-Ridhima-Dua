At Animal Watch 365, our Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Trackers
At Animal Watch 365, our Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Trackers offer a lightweight, 
power-efficient solution for monitoring animals within close to medium-range environments. 
These trackers are ideal for farms, kennels, shelters, 
and indoor livestock operations where GPS may be excessive or unavailable. 
BLE technology enables accurate proximity detection and interaction with nearby 
Bluetooth-enabled devices, all while conserving battery life for extended operation.

Our products undergo rigorous quality control, are built on the latest research, 
and are supported by a team focused on customer satisfaction. 
Whether you’re managing dozens of animals or scaling to hundreds, 
our BLE tracking systems provide a smart, 
reliable foundation for real-time awareness and operational efficiency.
