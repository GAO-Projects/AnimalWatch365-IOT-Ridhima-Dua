Case Studies
US Florida Hog Farm
A commercial hog operation in central Florida adopted cellular 
trackers to monitor animal behavior and heat cycles. 
The result: a 22% improvement in breeding timing accuracy and 
reduced vet visits thanks to earlier health alerts

US Missouri Cattle Operation
A large-scale cattle ranch in Missouri used our 4G-enabled trackers 
to oversee herds during rotational grazing and interstate transport. 
Location data streamlined reporting, and the team reduced losses during drives by 90%.

CA Ontario Equine Facility
An equine boarding and training center in Ontario implemented 3G-enabled trackers to 
monitor horses prone to anxiety and escape. Real-time alerts allowed staff to intervene instantly, 
improving safety and trust with horse owners
