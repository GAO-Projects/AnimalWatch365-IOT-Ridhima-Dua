Key Features & Functionalities
Multi-Mile Range: Track animals up to 10+ miles from gateways
Energy Efficiency: Low power use for months or even years of runtime
Geofencing Alerts: Custom boundary notifications for animal containment
Environmental Monitoring: Track ambient temperature and activity levels
Offline Logging: Data storage during disconnection, auto-synced on reconnection
High Capacity: Scalable to thousands of animals without significant infrastructure cost
