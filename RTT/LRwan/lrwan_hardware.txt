LoRaWAN End Devices are ideal for robust, energy-efficient animal

Built-in GPS, accelerometer, and temperature sensors –

Solar-assisted or rechargeable battery options – 
Solar Power components support sustainable energy solutions for remote, 
maintenance-free tracker operation.

Long-range antennas and LoRaWAN gateways – 
LoRaWAN Gateways enable extended range and connectivity for wide-area 
livestock monitoring in rural or expansive farms.
