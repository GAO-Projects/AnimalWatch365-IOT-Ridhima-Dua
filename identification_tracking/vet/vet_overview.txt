Visual Ear Tags for Reliable Animal Identification
Visual Ear Tags are a time-tested and cost-effective 
identification solution used widely across pet and livestock 
management operations. At Animal Watch 365 Technologies, 
we provide customizable, high-visibility ear tags that support 
herd health programs, biosecurity compliance, and rapid visual 
identification in field conditions. Whether managing thousands 
of cattle or keeping track of high-value animals, these tags 
allow farmers, veterinarians, and regulatory agencies to visually 
recognize individual animals at a glance. Headquartered in Houston, 
TX, we bring leading-edge durability, clear printing, and traceability 
features to every tag we produce. Our visual ear tags are trusted by 
customers across North America who value rugged products backed by 
responsive service and innovation-driven development.
